# Other

-- Multiple Symptoms / Not Sure What Category Checklist --
1. Which problem do you notice first when you start driving — or do they all show up at the same time?
2. Are the issues happening together every time, or does each one come and go on its own?
3. Did everything start around the same time, or did one problem show up first and the others followed later?
4. Have you noticed any pattern — like it only happens when it rains, when the car is cold, when you turn, or at certain speeds?
5. Is there one symptom that worries you the most, or feels the most urgent?
6. Has anything changed recently — like a long road trip, towing something, an oil change, or new tires?
7. Have you had any dashboard warning lights come on, even briefly?

-- After a Recent Accident or Impact Checklist --
1. When did the accident or impact happen, and have you driven the car since?
2. Was it a collision with another vehicle, a curb hit, a pothole, or running over something in the road?
3. Did any airbags deploy, or did any warning lights come on after the impact?
4. Are you filing an insurance claim, or is this something you're handling on your own?
5. Does the steering feel different — pulling to one side, off-center, or shaky?
6. Are you seeing any new fluid drips on the ground where you park?
7. Does the car feel like it's sitting level, or does one corner look lower than the others?

-- After Recent Service or Repair Work Checklist --
1. Where was the recent work done — at our shop, a dealership, or somewhere else?
2. About how long ago was that service, and do you have the receipt or invoice handy?
3. What was the original reason you took it in — and is this the same problem coming back, or something new?
4. Did the issue show up right after picking up the car, or did it appear days or weeks later?
5. Are any parts or labor still under warranty from that previous shop?
6. Has the car been driven much since the work was done?
7. Did the other shop mention anything they recommended but didn't end up doing?

-- Safety Concern — Don't Feel Safe Driving It Checklist --
1. Are you currently somewhere safe, like home or a parking lot, or are you stranded on the road?
2. Is the car drivable at all, or is it not starting or not moving?
3. Are you seeing smoke, steam, or smelling something burning?
4. Are the brakes working normally, or do they feel soft, low, or like they're not stopping the car?
5. Is the steering working normally, or does it feel stiff, loose, or hard to control?
6. Is there a flashing warning light on the dashboard right now, like a flashing check engine or red temperature light?
7. Would you feel comfortable driving it slowly to the shop, or would you rather have it towed in?

-- General Check-Up or Pre-Trip Inspection Checklist --
1. Are you preparing for a long road trip, or is this more of a routine peace-of-mind check?
2. About when was the last time the car had any maintenance done on it?
3. Are there any small things you've noticed but haven't worried about — like a quiet noise or a soft feel?
4. About how many miles are on the car right now?
5. Do you have any service records or a maintenance schedule from the manufacturer you'd like us to follow?
6. Are there any specific areas you want us to focus on, like brakes, tires, or fluids?
7. Is there a date you need the car ready by?

-- Car Has Been Sitting Unused for a Long Time Checklist --
1. About how long has the car been sitting without being driven?
2. Was it parked in a garage, under a cover, or outside in the weather?
3. Did you take any steps before parking it — like adding fuel stabilizer or disconnecting the battery?
4. Have you tried to start it recently, and if so, did it start up or struggle?
5. Is the car a hybrid or electric vehicle?
6. Have you noticed any leaks, stains, or puddles where it's been parked?
7. Do you want it picked up by a tow truck, or have you been able to get it running enough to drive in?
