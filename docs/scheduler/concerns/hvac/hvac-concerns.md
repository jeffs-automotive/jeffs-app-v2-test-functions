# Hvac

-- AC blows warm or hot air Checklist --
1. Does the AC blow warm air all the time, or does it cool at first and then warm up after a few minutes of driving?
   - Warm all the time=always_warm | Cools first, then warms up=cools_then_warms | Not sure=unsure
2. When you turn the AC on, do you hear a click from under the hood like something is kicking in?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the AC work better when you're driving on the highway versus sitting at a stoplight?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Have you noticed any oily or wet spots on the ground under the front of the car?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
5. Has the AC ever been recharged, or had any work done on it in the last year or two?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the warm air start suddenly one day, or did the cooling get weaker little by little over time?
   - Suddenly=sudden | Gradually over time=gradual | Not sure=unsure
7. Does it blow warm from every vent (dash, floor, and defrost), or just some of them?
   - Every vent=every | Just some=some | Not sure=unsure

-- AC is weak (not cold enough) Checklist --
1. Is the air at least somewhat cool, just not as cold as it used to be?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the air get colder when you press the recirculate or "max AC" button?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Has the cabin air filter been changed in the last year or two?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Does the AC cool better when the car is moving versus when you're parked?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you noticed any sweet or chemical smell along with the weak cooling?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the cooling slowly get worse over a season, or did it drop off all at once?
   - Suddenly=sudden | Gradually over time=gradual | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the system feel weak on hot, humid days but okay on cooler days?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Cold air=cold | Room-temperature air=room_temp | Just a little warm=little_warm | Not sure=unsure
2. Does it take a long time of driving before any warm air comes out, or does it never warm up at all?
   - Takes a long time=long_time | Never warms up=never | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the temperature gauge on the dash get up to its normal spot, or does it stay cold?
   - Reaches normal=normal | Stays cold=cold | Not sure=unsure
4. Have you needed to add coolant or antifreeze recently, or noticed the coolant tank running low?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
5. Have you seen any puddles or wet spots under the front of the car?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
6. Is the heat the same on the driver and passenger side, or different?
   - Same on both sides=same | Different=different | Not sure=unsure
7. Did the heat problem start after the car sat for a while, or after any recent service?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Vents don't blow strongly (weak airflow) Checklist --
1. Is the air weak on every fan speed, or only on certain speeds (like only working on high)?
   - Weak on every speed=every | Only on certain speeds=certain | Not sure=unsure
2. When was the cabin air filter last replaced?
   - In the last year=lt_1_year | 1 to 3 years ago=1_to_3 | More than 3 years ago=gt_3 | Never / not sure=unsure
3. [multi] Is the airflow weak from every vent, or only the dash, floor, or defrost?
   - Every vent=every | Dash vents=dash | Floor vents=floor | Defrost vents=defrost | Not sure=unsure
4. Does the air come out stronger when you switch to recirculate?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you heard any squeaking, grinding, or rattling from behind the dashboard or passenger footwell?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the weak airflow start suddenly, or did it slowly drop off over months?
   - Suddenly=sudden | Gradually over time=gradual | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the fan come on at all when you turn it to the lowest speed?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Clicking=clicking | Whistling=whistling | Grinding=grinding | Rattling=rattling | Buzzing=buzzing | Not sure=unsure

   - Only when fan is on=fan_on | Also when fan is off=fan_off_too | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the noise change when you raise or lower the fan speed?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Does the noise change when you switch the vents between dash, floor, and defrost?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Does the noise change when you switch between fresh air and recirculate?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the noise start after leaves, debris, or anything got near the cowl at the base of the windshield?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Is the noise coming from behind the dash, the passenger footwell, or under the hood?
   - Behind the dash=dash | Passenger footwell=footwell | Under the hood=hood | Not sure=unsure

-- Bad smell from vents (musty / sweet / other) Checklist --
1. How would you describe the smell: musty/moldy, sweet like maple syrup, gasoline, burning, or something else?
   - Musty/moldy=musty | Sweet like maple syrup=sweet | Gasoline=gas | Burning=burning | Something else=other | Not sure=unsure
2. Is the smell strongest when you first turn the AC on, or after the AC has been running a while?
   - When first turned on=first | After running a while=after | Not sure=unsure
3. [multi] Does the smell happen with the AC on, the heat on, or both?
   - AC on=ac | Heat on=heat | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
4. Have the windows been fogging up at the same time the smell shows up?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Has the cabin air filter been changed in the last year or two?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the smell start after the car sat unused for a while, or after a recent service?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the smell go away if you switch to recirculate, or get worse?
   - Goes away=away | Gets worse=worse | No change=no_change | Not sure=unsure

-- One zone works but another doesn't Checklist --
1. [multi] Which side or zone is the problem: driver, passenger, or rear?
   - Driver=driver | Passenger=passenger | Rear=rear | Not sure=unsure
2. Is one side blowing cold while the other blows warm, or one warm while the other is cold?
   - One cold / other warm=cold_warm | One warm / other cold=warm_cold | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the problem happen only with AC, only with heat, or both?
   - Only AC=ac_only | Only heat=heat_only | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
4. Have you heard any clicking or tapping sound from behind the dashboard when you change the temperature setting?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Does the temperature on the bad side change at all when you adjust its dial, or does it stay stuck no matter what?
   - Changes some=some | Stays stuck=stuck | Not sure=unsure
6. Did the problem start after the car sat in very cold or very hot weather?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the airflow strength feel normal on the bad side, even if the temperature is wrong?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
