# Noise

-- Engine Ticking or Tapping Checklist --
1. Does the ticking start the moment you turn the key, or does it only show up after the engine has been running for a few minutes?
2. Does the speed of the ticking change as you press the gas pedal?
3. Is the sound coming from the top of the engine or the lower part of the engine?
4. When was the last time you had the oil changed?
5. Have you noticed the oil pressure warning light flicker on, even briefly?
6. Does the ticking get quieter or go away once the engine warms up?
7. Does the noise get louder when the engine is working hard, like going up a hill or carrying heavy loads?

-- Clunking Over Bumps Checklist --
1. Does the clunk happen every time you hit a bump, or only with big bumps and potholes?
2. Is the sound coming from the front of the vehicle, the back, or both?
3. Does the clunk happen on just one side of the car, or both sides equally?
4. Have you noticed the vehicle feeling bouncy or unsettled after going over bumps?
5. Does the clunk also happen when you start moving from a stop or when you come to a stop?
6. Have you hit any large potholes, curbs, or road debris recently?
7. Does the noise happen at any speed, or only at low speeds?

-- Humming or Whirring at Speed Checklist --
1. Does the hum get louder the faster you drive?
2. Does the noise change when you turn the steering wheel left versus right?
3. Does the sound seem to come from one specific wheel area, or is it hard to pin down?
4. Does the hum stay the same when you take your foot off the gas and coast?
5. Have you had new tires put on recently, or are your tires worn unevenly?
6. Does the noise feel like it's coming through the floor or the seat as a vibration too?
7. Does the hum disappear or get quieter when you're stopped at a light?

-- High-Pitched Whining Under the Hood Checklist --
1. Does the whine speed up and slow down along with the engine speed?
2. Does the whine get louder when you turn the steering wheel, especially when parking?
3. Does it happen mostly when the engine is cold, when it's warm, or all the time?
4. Does the whine get worse in cold or damp weather?
5. Have you noticed the battery warning light or dim headlights along with the noise?
6. Does the whine come from the front of the engine area where the belts are?
7. Did the noise start suddenly, or has it been getting worse gradually?

-- Rattling Underneath the Car Checklist --
1. Does the rattle happen mostly at startup, when accelerating, or at idle?
2. Does the sound change or stop when you go over bumps versus smooth road?
3. Is the rattle more of a tinny sound, like a can with a rock in it, or more of a heavy clang?
4. Does it get worse when the engine is revved up?
5. Have you driven over anything in the road recently or scraped the underside of the car?
6. Does the noise come from the front, middle, or rear underside of the vehicle?
7. Does the rattle quiet down once you're at cruising speed on the highway?

-- Hissing Noise Checklist --
1. Does the hiss happen with the engine off after you've shut the car down, or only when running?
2. Does the noise stop when you turn off the air conditioning?
3. Have you noticed the engine running rough, idling unevenly, or a warning light coming on?
4. Is the temperature gauge running higher than normal, or have you seen steam from under the hood?
5. Does the hiss seem to come from under the hood, from underneath the car, or from the dashboard area?
6. Does the air conditioning still blow cold, or has it gotten weaker?
7. Have you topped off coolant or refrigerant recently?

-- Popping or Clicking When Turning Checklist --
1. Does the popping happen mostly during sharp turns, like in parking lots, or also during gentle turns?
2. Is the noise louder when turning one direction versus the other?
3. Does the popping get faster and louder the tighter you turn the wheel?
4. Does it happen when going forward, in reverse, or both?
5. Have you noticed any grease splattered on the back of your wheel or inside of your tire?
6. Does the noise happen even when you're not turning, or only during turns?
7. Have you hit a deep pothole or scraped a curb recently?

-- Deep Knocking from the Engine Checklist --
1. Does the knock happen the moment you start the engine, or does it take a few minutes to show up?
2. Does the knocking get worse when you accelerate or are climbing a hill?
3. Does it get louder or quieter when the engine warms up?
4. What grade of gasoline have you been using, and does your owner's manual recommend a higher grade?
5. Have you noticed any warning lights on the dashboard, especially the oil pressure light or check engine light?
6. Is the knocking a deep, heavy thumping or a lighter, faster tapping?
7. Have you been low on oil recently, or has it been a long time since the last oil change?

-- Squeaking or Creaking Over Bumps Checklist --
1. Is the squeak worse when the car is cold in the morning, or when it's warmed up?
2. Does the squeak only happen over bumps, or also when you turn the steering wheel while sitting still?
3. Does it sound like dry rubber being twisted, or more like metal rubbing on metal?
4. Does the noise come from one corner of the car, or all around?
5. Does the squeak happen at low speeds only, or also on the highway?
6. Has the car been sitting outside in cold or wet weather a lot?
7. Does the squeak get worse when carrying passengers or heavy loads?

-- Electrical Buzzing Checklist --
1. Does the buzzing keep going even after you turn the engine off, or does it stop?
2. Does the sound seem to come from the dashboard, behind the dash, or from under the hood?
3. Does the buzz happen only when certain things are turned on, like the headlights, blower fan, or turn signals?
4. Have you noticed the headlights or dashboard lights flickering or dimming?
5. Have you had any electrical work, accessories, or aftermarket items installed recently?
6. Does the battery seem weak, or does the car sometimes have trouble starting?
7. Does the buzzing happen all the time, or only at certain temperatures or weather conditions?
