# Warning Light

-- Check Engine Light Checklist --
1. Is the light flashing/blinking or just steady on?
2. Does the engine feel rough, like it's shaking, hesitating, or losing power?
3. Have you noticed any unusual smells, especially something that smells like rotten eggs or burning?
4. Did the light come on right after you filled up with gas? Did you check that your gas cap is tight?
5. Is the car using more gas than usual, or is there any black smoke coming out of the tailpipe?
6. About how long has the light been on, and does it ever turn itself off and come back on later?
7. Have you noticed any clicking, ticking, or popping sounds from the engine while the light is on?

-- Service Engine Soon / Maintenance Required Light Checklist --
1. Does the message on your dash say "Service Engine Soon," "Maintenance Required," or "Service Due" — anything that sounds like a reminder rather than an alarm?
2. About how many miles has it been since your last oil change or scheduled service?
3. Does the car feel and drive completely normal otherwise?
4. Did the light come on at a round-number mileage, like right at 5,000 or 75,000 miles?
5. Is there a separate check engine light on too, or is this the only warning showing?
6. Has anyone reset this reminder for you recently after a service?

-- Battery / Charging Light Checklist --
1. Did the light come on suddenly while you were driving, or did it gradually start showing up?
2. When you turn the car off and try to start it again, does it crank slowly, click, or not start at all?
3. Have you noticed your headlights or dashboard lights getting dimmer, especially at idle or when you turn other things on?
4. Are your power windows, radio, or wipers running slower or acting weird?
5. Have you had to jump-start the car recently, or have you replaced the battery in the last couple of years?
6. Do you hear any squealing or whining sound from under the hood when the light is on?
7. Does the light go off when you rev the engine, or does it stay on no matter what?

-- Oil Pressure Light Checklist --
1. Did you pull over and shut the engine off when the light came on, or have you been driving with it on?
2. Have you checked the oil level on the dipstick? If yes, was it low, empty, or normal?
3. When was your last oil change, and do you know if the car has been burning or leaking oil between changes?
4. Do you hear any ticking, tapping, or knocking noises from the engine when it's running?
5. Did the light come on suddenly, or does it flicker on and off — maybe at idle or when stopping at a light?
6. Have you noticed any oil spots on your driveway or garage floor?
7. Does the light come on and stay on, or does it go away once you start driving?

-- Engine Temperature Light Checklist --
1. Is the temperature gauge reading high or in the red zone, or is the gauge normal but the light is still on?
2. Have you seen any steam or smoke coming from under the hood?
3. Have you checked the coolant reservoir? Is it full, low, or completely empty?
4. Have you had to add coolant or water to the car recently, or noticed green, orange, or pink puddles where you park?
5. Does the heater inside the car still blow hot, or does it blow cold air now?
6. Did the light come on after sitting in heavy traffic, climbing a hill, or pulling a load?
7. Have you continued driving with the light on, or did you stop right away?

-- TPMS / Tire Pressure Light Checklist --
1. Has it been noticeably colder outside recently — like a cold morning or the first chilly day of the season?
2. Is the light steady on, or is it flashing on and off?
3. Do any of your tires look visibly low or flat compared to the others?
4. Have you noticed the car pulling to one side, riding rougher, or feeling slower to respond?
5. Did you recently have tires rotated, replaced, or air added?
6. Have you driven over any potholes, debris, curbs, or noticed a slow leak in any tire?
7. Does the light go off after you've been driving for a while, or does it stay on the whole trip?

-- ABS / Anti-Lock Brake Light Checklist --
1. Are the regular brakes still working normally when you press the pedal — stopping the car like usual?
2. Is the red BRAKE light on too, or is it just the yellow ABS light?
3. When you brake hard or on a slippery surface, do you feel pulsing or vibrating in the pedal like normal, or nothing at all?
4. Have you noticed the car pulling to one side when braking, or one wheel locking up?
5. Did the light come on right after driving through deep water, a car wash, or hitting a big pothole or curb?
6. Have you had any brake work, tire work, or wheel bearing work done recently?
7. Does the light come on every time you start the car, or only sometimes while driving?

-- Brake System (Red) Light Checklist --
1. First thing — is your parking brake or emergency brake fully released?
2. Does the brake pedal feel different — softer, spongy, sinking to the floor, or harder than normal?
3. Have you checked the brake fluid reservoir under the hood? Was the level near the MIN line or below?
4. Is the yellow ABS light on at the same time, or is it just the red brake light?
5. Does the car still stop normally when you press the brake, or does it take longer than usual?
6. Have you noticed any fluid leaking near any of the wheels or in the spot where you park?
7. Did the light come on suddenly, or did it start coming on and going off before staying on?

-- Airbag / SRS Light Checklist --
1. Has the car been in any kind of accident, collision, or hard bump recently — even a minor one?
2. Has anyone done work on the seats, dashboard, steering wheel, or seat belts recently?
3. Is there anything stuck in any seat belt buckle — a coin, a crumb, a piece of plastic?
4. Did you recently have a car seat installed or use the front passenger seat occupancy area differently than usual?
5. Does the light flash a pattern of blinks, or is it just on steady?
6. Has the car been sitting unused for a long time, or has the battery been disconnected or replaced recently?
7. Did the light come on right after driving through a flooded area or getting the interior wet?

-- Traction Control / Stability Light Checklist --
1. Is the light on steady all the time, or does it only flash briefly when the road is slippery?
2. Is the ABS light on at the same time, or just the traction/stability light?
3. Have you noticed the car feeling slippery, losing grip, or wheels spinning when you don't expect it?
4. Did the light come on after driving in snow, rain, mud, or off-road?
5. Have you recently put on new tires, especially a different size or only replaced one or two?
6. Did you accidentally press the traction-control button to turn the system off?
7. Does the steering feel heavier, or have you noticed any other warning lights joining this one?

-- Power Steering / EPS Light Checklist --
1. Is the steering wheel harder to turn than usual, especially at low speeds or when parking?
2. Does the steering feel heavy all the time, or only when the light is on?
3. Have you heard any whining or groaning sound when turning the wheel?
4. Did you notice the light come on right after starting the car, or did it come on while you were already driving?
5. Has your battery been weak, dead, or recently replaced?
6. Are there any reddish-pink fluid spots where you park (only applies if your car uses hydraulic power steering, not all do)?
7. Does the light come on and go off on its own, or does it stay on the whole time?

-- Multiple Warning Lights At Once Checklist --
1. Which lights are on — can you describe the colors and shapes, or read what they say?
2. Did all the lights come on at the same time, or did they show up one after another?
3. Is the car running rough, losing power, or do the headlights and dashboard look dimmer than normal?
4. When you try to start the car, does it crank slowly, click, or struggle?
5. Have you noticed any burning smell or smoke from under the hood?
6. Has the battery been replaced recently, or have you had any electrical work done on the car?
7. Does anything electrical inside the car act weird — radio resetting, gauges jumping around, windows slow, dome light flickering?
