# Smell

-- Sweet Smell (Maple Syrup / Antifreeze) Checklist --
1. Do you smell it more when the heater or defroster is running?
2. Is the smell stronger inside the cabin or outside under the hood?
3. Have you noticed any damp spots or wet patches on the passenger-side floor or carpet?
4. Does the windshield fog up on the inside even when the weather is dry?
5. Have you had to add coolant or antifreeze to the vehicle recently?
6. Does the smell come and go, or is it there every time you drive?
7. Do you see any green, orange, or pink fluid leaking under the car when it sits?

-- Burnt Oil Smell Checklist --
1. Do you smell it most when the engine has been running hard or after a long drive?
2. Does the smell come from under the hood, from underneath the car, or through the vents?
3. Have you seen any blue or gray smoke coming from the back of the car or from under the hood?
4. Have you noticed oil drops or oil spots on the ground where you park?
5. Has the oil light on the dash come on, or have you had to top off the oil between changes?
6. Does the smell get stronger right after you turn the engine off and walk away?
7. Have you had any recent oil changes or engine work done?

-- Gasoline / Fuel Smell Checklist --
1. Do you smell it most right after starting the car, while driving, or after parking?
2. Is the smell stronger inside the cabin or outside near the back of the car?
3. Have you noticed any wet spots or puddles under the vehicle where it sits?
4. Did you recently fill up the tank, and did the pump click off normally?
5. Is your gas cap on tight, and does it click when you close it?
6. Has the check-engine light come on?
7. Does the smell get worse when you're driving uphill, accelerating hard, or sitting at idle?

-- Rotten Egg / Sulfur Smell Checklist --
1. Do you smell it mostly from the tailpipe area, or is it inside the cabin too?
2. Does it happen more after hard driving or sitting in traffic for a while?
3. Has the check-engine light come on or been on recently?
4. Is the car running rough, hesitating, or losing power when you smell it?
5. Have you noticed the smell more after filling up at a particular gas station or with a different brand of fuel?
6. Have you had any work done on the exhaust, catalytic converter, or emissions system?
7. Is the smell present right at startup, or only once the engine has warmed up?

-- Burning Electrical / Plastic Smell Checklist --
1. Have you noticed any flickering lights, blown fuses, or dashboard warnings around the same time?
2. Does the smell get worse when you turn on the heater, AC, or fan?
3. Is the smell coming from the dashboard area, the vents, or under the hood?
4. Have any electrical accessories or aftermarket parts been installed recently?
5. Does the smell come on when you use a specific feature like the radio, seat warmers, or power windows?
6. Have you seen any smoke or haze inside the cabin?
7. Does the smell stay even after the car is turned off and cooled down?

-- Burning Rubber / Hot Brake Smell Checklist --
1. Do you smell it more after stopping the car, especially after coming down a hill or heavy braking?
2. Is the smell coming from one specific wheel or all four?
3. Does the parking brake release fully, and have you been able to confirm it's all the way off?
4. Have you noticed any squealing, grinding, or dragging feeling from the brakes?
5. Does the smell happen after long highway drives even when you haven't braked much?
6. Have you seen any smoke or haze coming from a wheel area or from under the hood?
7. Does the steering feel heavier or different when the smell is present?

-- Musty / Mildew Smell from Vents Checklist --
1. Does the smell only come through the vents when you turn on the AC or heater?
2. Is the smell strongest in the first few seconds after you turn the fan on, then fade?
3. Does the smell go away when you switch to outside-air mode versus recirculate?
4. Have you noticed any water dripping under the dashboard onto your feet?
5. When was the cabin air filter last changed, if you know?
6. Is the car parked outside often, or does it sit unused for long stretches?
7. Have the carpets or seats been wet recently from a spill, leak, or open window in the rain?

-- Exhaust Fumes Inside the Cabin Checklist --
1. Do you smell the exhaust more when the windows are up, or only with a window cracked open?
2. Does the smell get worse when you're stopped at a light versus when you're driving?
3. Does it come on stronger when the heater or fan is running, especially on recirculate mode?
4. Have you noticed the car running louder than normal, like a rumble or hissing sound?
5. Have you felt lightheaded, dizzy, drowsy, or had a headache while driving?
6. Has anyone done recent work on the exhaust, muffler, or undercarriage?
7. Is the rear hatch, trunk seal, or any window seal damaged or leaking air?
