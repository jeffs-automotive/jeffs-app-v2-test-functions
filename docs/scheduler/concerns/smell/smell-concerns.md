# Smell

-- Sweet smell (maple syrup / antifreeze) Checklist --
1. Do you smell it more when the heater or defroster is running?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Is the smell stronger inside the cabin or outside under the hood?
   - Inside the cabin=cabin | Outside / under the hood=hood | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
3. Have you noticed any damp spots or wet patches on the passenger-side floor or carpet?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
4. Does the windshield fog up on the inside even when the weather is dry?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you had to add coolant or antifreeze to the vehicle recently?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does the smell come and go, or is it there every time you drive?
   - Every time I drive=every | Comes and goes=intermittent | Not sure=unsure
7. Do you see any green, orange, or pink fluid leaking under the car when it sits?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
