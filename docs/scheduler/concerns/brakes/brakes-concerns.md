# Brakes

-- High-Pitched Squealing Checklist --
1. Does it occur at high speeds, low speeds, or right before stopping?
2. Does it quiet down or get louder when pressing the pedal harder?
3. Is the noise worse during the first few stops in the morning or does it get loiuder the longer you drive?
4. Does rain, high humidity, or morning dew affect the sound?
5. Have you had any brake work done recently?
6. Does the noise happen after the vehicle sits for a while and then goes away after driving?
7. Do you hear the noise coming from the front or rear of the vehicle? Left or right side?

-- Metallic Grinding Checklist --
1. Does the grinding happen every single time you apply the brakes?
2. Do you hear a scraping sound even when your foot is off the pedal?
3. Does the sound feel like it is coming from the front or rear? Left or right side?
4. Can you feel a harsh grinding sensation through the floor or pedal?
5. Did this sound start suddenly, or build up over several weeks?
6. Do you feel safe driving the vehicle?
7. Have you had brake work done recently?

-- Spongy or Soft Pedal Checklist --
1. Does the brake pedal get firmer if you pump it rapidly three times?
2. Can you easily push the pedal all the way down to the carpet?
3. Does the vehicle take longer to start slowing down than it used to?
4. Have you noticed the brake fluid reservoir level dropping recently?
5. Has the brake system been opened or bled for service recently?

-- Pedal Sinks to Floor Checklist --
1. Does the pedal creep down while holding pressure at a red light?
2. Does it sink faster if you press lightly or if you press firmly?
3. Are there any visible fluid spots on your driveway or garage floor?
4. Are there any warning lights on the dash?
5. Does the pedal pop right back up instantly when you release your foot?
6. Have you had any brake work done recently?

-- Pulsating or Vibrating Pedal Checklist -- 
1. At what specific speed does the foot pedal vibration become noticeable?
2. Does the pulsation get worse the harder you press on the brakes?
3. Do you feel the vibration in the steering hweel or in your seat?
4. Does the pulsation worsen after driving down a long hill or mountain?
5. Do you feel the vibration all the time, when first driving or after driving for a while?
6. Have you had brake work done recently?

-- Hard or Unresponsive Pedal Checklist -- 
1. Is the pedal stiff before you turn the engine key on in the morning?
2. Does the pedal drop slightly when you crank the engine over?
3. Does the pedal get harder to press the longer you drive the vehicle?
4. Do you hear any noises while you are braking?
5. Does the engine idle rough or stumble when you press the brakes?
6. Have you had brake workj done recently?
