# Brakes

-- High-pitched squealing Checklist --
1. Does it occur at high speeds, low speeds, or right before stopping?
   - High speeds=high | Low speeds=low | Right before stopping=stopping | Not sure=unsure
2. Does it quiet down or get louder when pressing the pedal harder?
   - Quieter=quieter | Louder=louder | No change=no_change | Not sure=unsure
3. Is the noise worse during the first few stops in the morning or does it get louder the longer you drive?
   - Worse first stops in the morning=morning | Louder the longer I drive=longer | About the same=same | Not sure=unsure
4. Does rain, high humidity, or morning dew affect the sound?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you had any brake work done recently?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does the noise happen after the vehicle sits for a while and then goes away after driving?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. [multi] Do you hear the noise coming from the front or rear of the vehicle? Left or right side?
   - Front=front | Rear=rear | Left side=left | Right side=right | All four wheels=all | Not sure=unsure
