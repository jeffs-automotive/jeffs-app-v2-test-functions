# Steering

-- Hard to Turn / Heavy Steering Checklist --
1. Is it harder to turn the wheel at low speeds and parking, or also at higher speeds?
2. Did this come on suddenly overnight, or has it gotten gradually worse over days or weeks?
3. Is it equally hard to turn in both directions, or worse turning one way than the other?
4. Have you noticed any red or pink fluid spots under the front of the car where you park?
5. Do you hear any whining, groaning, or humming sound while turning?
6. Does your car have power steering you can feel quitting, or has the wheel always felt this stiff since you got it?
7. Has the battery been dying or have any warning lights been on the dashboard recently?

-- Loose or Sloppy Steering Checklist --
1. Can you wiggle the steering wheel a little bit side-to-side before the car actually starts to turn?
2. Do you find yourself constantly making small corrections to keep the car going straight?
3. Does the car feel floaty or disconnected from the road, like it's not really tracking where you point it?
4. Have you hit any large potholes, curbs, or had a fender-bender recently?
5. Are your front tires wearing more on the inside or outside edges than in the middle?
6. Does the looseness feel worse at higher speeds, lower speeds, or about the same all the time?
7. About how many miles are on the car, and do you know roughly when the front-end parts were last looked at?

-- Steering Wheel Off-Center When Driving Straight Checklist --
1. When the car is going straight down a flat road, is the steering wheel tilted left or right of center?
2. Did this start right after a recent alignment, tire rotation, or other suspension work?
3. Have you hit a curb, pothole, or had any kind of impact to the front of the car recently?
4. Does the car still drive straight, or does it also pull to one side along with the wheel being crooked?
5. Have any tires been replaced recently, and if so were all four done or just some of them?
6. Are all four tires the same brand, model, and roughly the same age?
7. Do you remember when you last had the tire pressures checked on all four corners?

-- Noise When Turning the Steering Wheel Checklist --
1. What does the sound feel like — a whine or hum, a clicking or popping, a creak, or a clunk?
2. Does the noise happen mostly at low speeds and parking, or also at higher speeds?
3. Is it louder when you turn the wheel all the way to one side and hold it there?
4. Does the noise happen even when the car isn't moving, just turning the wheel while parked?
5. Does it sound like it's coming from the front wheels, the engine bay, or somewhere underneath?
6. Have you checked the power steering fluid level recently, or do you know if it's low?
7. Does the noise change or go away in cold weather versus warm weather?

-- Steering Wheel Shakes at Highway Speed Checklist --
1. At what speed does the shake start, and does it get worse the faster you go or eventually smooth back out?
2. Does the shake happen all the time at that speed, or only when you press the brakes?
3. If you briefly let go of the wheel at highway speed, does the shake continue or quiet down?
4. Is the whole car shaking, or is it really just the steering wheel in your hands?
5. When were your tires last balanced or rotated?
6. Have you recently lost a wheel weight or hit something that could have knocked a tire out of balance?
7. Are any of the tires showing uneven wear, scalloped patches, or bald spots?

-- Pulling, Drifting, or Wandering on the Road Checklist --
1. Does the car pull steadily to one specific side, or does it wander back and forth between lanes?
2. Which direction does it pull — always left, always right, or it changes?
3. Does the pull happen on flat roads too, or mostly on roads that slope to one side?
4. Does it pull harder when you press the brakes, when you accelerate, or about the same regardless?
5. When was the last time the tires were rotated, replaced, or had pressures checked?
6. Have you been in a recent accident, hit a big pothole, or run over a curb?
7. Have you had an alignment done recently, and did the problem start before or after that?

-- Clunking, Knocking, or Rough Ride Over Bumps Checklist --
1. Does the noise happen every time you go over a bump, or only over bigger ones?
2. Does the front of the car keep bouncing two or three times after a bump instead of settling right away?
3. Does the front end dip down hard when you brake, or does the back end squat down hard when you accelerate?
4. Does the car lean or sway a lot when you go around corners or change lanes quickly?
5. Where does the clunking seem to come from — front left, front right, or the back of the car?
6. Have you noticed any oily or wet streaks running down the metal posts behind the front wheels?
7. About how many miles are on the car, and have the shocks or suspension parts ever been replaced?
