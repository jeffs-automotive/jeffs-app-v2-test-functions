# Performance

-- Hesitation or Lag When Accelerating Checklist --
1. Does the hesitation happen when you first press the gas, or only when you push it hard for passing or merging?
2. Does it happen in every gear, or only in a certain speed range?
3. Is the check engine light on or flashing when it happens?
4. Did this start suddenly, or has it been getting worse over weeks or months?
5. Have you filled up at a different gas station recently or noticed it after a fuel-up?
6. Does it ever feel like the car jerks, bucks, or stumbles when you push the pedal?
7. Does the problem happen all the time or only sometimes?

-- Rough Idle or Shaking at a Stop Checklist --
1. Does the shaking happen only when you're stopped, or also when you're driving?
2. Does the shaking get better, worse, or go away when you shift into Neutral or Park?
3. Does turning on the A/C, heater, or defrost make the shaking worse?
4. Is the check engine light on, flashing, or has it come on recently?
5. Do you smell any gas fumes or rotten-egg smell from the exhaust when it's shaking?
6. Does the RPM needle bounce up and down on its own while you're sitting at a light?
7. When did you last have spark plugs or a tune-up done?

-- Stalling at Idle or When Stopping Checklist --
1. Does the engine die right as you come to a stop, or after sitting still for a few seconds?
2. Does it stall more often when the A/C, heater, or headlights are on?
3. Will it restart right away after it stalls, or do you have to wait?
4. Does it stall when the engine is cold, after it warms up, or both?
5. Is the check engine light on when this happens?
6. Have you noticed a rough or unstable idle leading up to the stall?
7. Does the stalling happen more in hot weather, cold weather, or no pattern?

-- Stalling While Driving (Under Load) Checklist --
1. Does it cut out at highway speed, while going uphill, or only at slow speeds?
2. Does it die suddenly with no warning, or does it sputter and lose power first?
3. After it dies, does it crank back over right away, or do you have to wait several minutes?
4. Does the dashboard go dark or do warning lights flash when it cuts out?
5. How much fuel was in the tank when this happened?
6. Have you noticed the temperature gauge running hotter than normal?
7. Is there any smoke, smell, or unusual noise right before it dies?

-- Hard to Start When Cold (After Sitting Overnight) Checklist --
1. Does it take several seconds of cranking before it finally fires up?
2. Once it starts, does it run rough for the first minute or so before smoothing out?
3. Is this only an issue when it's been cold outside, or does it happen any time it sits overnight?
4. Did you have to jump-start it, or did the battery sound strong while cranking?
5. Have you noticed any black smoke or strong gas smell when it finally starts?
6. About how long has it been doing this — days, weeks, or months?
7. Does the check engine light stay on after it starts, or come on and go off?

-- Hard to Start When Hot (Right After Driving) Checklist --
1. Does it only happen when you stop for a short errand and try to restart, like at a gas station?
2. Does it crank fine but take a long time to actually catch and run?
3. Have you noticed it after driving in hot weather or after sitting in traffic?
4. Does it start better if you press the gas pedal partway down while turning the key?
5. Once it does start, does it idle rough for a few seconds before smoothing out?
6. Is there any smell of raw gas around the engine when this happens?
7. Does it ever stall right after starting if you don't give it gas?

-- Low Power or Won't Accelerate Normally Checklist --
1. Is the loss of power constant, or does it come and go?
2. Does the engine rev up high but the car doesn't pick up speed like it used to?
3. Is the check engine light flashing, solid, or off?
4. Have you noticed a sudden drop in your gas mileage along with the power loss?
5. Does the car feel like it's stuck in a lower gear or "held back" when you accelerate?
6. Do you hear any unusual sounds — hissing, popping, or a louder-than-normal exhaust?
7. Does it happen more on hills, at highway speed, or all the time?

-- Surging or RPMs Going Up and Down on Their Own Checklist --
1. Does the surging happen at idle when you're stopped, or while you're driving at a steady speed?
2. Does the RPM needle visibly bounce up and down without you touching the pedal?
3. Does it surge more when the engine is cold, after it warms up, or both?
4. Does running the A/C or heat make the surging better or worse?
5. Is the check engine light on or has it flashed recently?
6. Have you had any recent work done on the throttle, intake, or air filter?
7. Does the car feel like it's lurching forward at low speeds even when your foot isn't on the gas?

-- Engine Misfire or Bucking Feeling Checklist --
1. Does it feel like the car is skipping, jerking, or kicking while you're driving?
2. Is the check engine light flashing when this happens? (Flashing is more serious than solid.)
3. Does the misfire happen at certain speeds, under hard acceleration, or randomly?
4. Does it get worse in rain, humidity, or wet weather?
5. Have you noticed the exhaust sound has changed — louder, popping, or uneven?
6. How long has it been since the spark plugs were replaced?
7. Does the misfire come and go, or is it constant once it starts?
