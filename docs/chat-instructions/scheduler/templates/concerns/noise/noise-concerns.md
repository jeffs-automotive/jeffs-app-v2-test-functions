# Noise

-- Engine ticking or tapping Checklist --
1. Does the ticking start the moment you turn the key, or does it only show up after the engine has been running for a few minutes?
   - Right when I turn the key=key | After a few minutes=few_minutes | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the speed of the ticking change as you press the gas pedal?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Is the sound coming from the top of the engine or the lower part of the engine?
   - Top of the engine=top | Lower part of the engine=lower | Not sure=unsure
4. When was the last time you had the oil changed?
   - In the last 3 months=lt_3_months | 3 to 6 months ago=3_to_6 | Over 6 months ago=gt_6 | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you noticed the oil pressure warning light flicker on, even briefly?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does the ticking get quieter or go away once the engine warms up?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the noise get louder when the engine is working hard, like going up a hill or carrying heavy loads?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Clunking over bumps Checklist --
1. Does the clunk happen every time you hit a bump, or only with big bumps and potholes?
   - Every bump=every | Only big bumps/potholes=big_only | Not sure=unsure
2. [multi] Is the sound coming from the front of the vehicle, the back, or both?
   - Front=front | Back=rear | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
3. [multi] Does the clunk happen on just one side of the car, or both sides equally?
   - Left side=left | Right side=right | Both sides equally=both | Not sure=unsure
4. Have you noticed the vehicle feeling bouncy or unsettled after going over bumps?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Does the clunk also happen when you start moving from a stop or when you come to a stop?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Have you hit any large potholes, curbs, or road debris recently?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the noise happen at any speed, or only at low speeds?
   - Any speed=any | Only at low speeds=low | Not sure=unsure

-- Humming or whirring at speed Checklist --
1. Does the hum get louder the faster you drive?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the noise change when you turn the steering wheel left versus right?
   - Louder turning left=left | Louder turning right=right | No change=no_change | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the sound seem to come from one specific wheel area, or is it hard to pin down?
   - Specific wheel=specific | Hard to pin down=vague | Not sure=unsure
4. Does the hum stay the same when you take your foot off the gas and coast?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you had new tires put on recently, or are your tires worn unevenly?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. [multi] Does the noise feel like it's coming through the floor or the seat as a vibration too?
   - Floor=floor | Seat=seat | Neither=neither | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the hum disappear or get quieter when you're stopped at a light?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the whine get louder when you turn the steering wheel, especially when parking?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Does it happen mostly when the engine is cold, when it's warm, or all the time?
   - When cold=cold | When warm=warm | All the time=all | Not sure=unsure
4. Does the whine get worse in cold or damp weather?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you noticed the battery warning light or dim headlights along with the noise?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does the whine come from the front of the engine area where the belts are?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Did the noise start suddenly, or has it been getting worse gradually?
   - Suddenly=sudden | Gradually over time=gradual | Not sure=unsure

-- Rattling underneath the car Checklist --
1. [multi] Does the rattle happen mostly at startup, when accelerating, or at idle?
   - At startup=startup | When accelerating=accel | At idle=idle | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the sound change or stop when you go over bumps versus smooth road?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Tinny / can with rock=tinny | Heavy clang=heavy | Not sure=unsure
4. Does it get worse when the engine is revved up?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you driven over anything in the road recently or scraped the underside of the car?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does the noise come from the front, middle, or rear underside of the vehicle?
   - Front=front | Middle=middle | Rear=rear | Not sure=unsure

-- Hissing noise Checklist --

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

2. Is the noise louder when turning one direction versus the other?
   - Louder turning left=left | Louder turning right=right | Same both directions=same | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the popping get faster and louder the tighter you turn the wheel?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. [multi] Does it happen when going forward, in reverse, or both?
   - Forward=forward | Reverse=reverse | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you noticed any grease splattered on the back of your wheel or inside of your tire?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
6. Does the noise happen even when you're not turning, or only during turns?
   - Only during turns=turns_only | Even when not turning=not_turning_too | Not sure=unsure

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Right at startup=startup | After a few minutes=few_minutes | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the knocking get worse when you accelerate or are climbing a hill?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Does it get louder or quieter when the engine warms up?
   - Louder=louder | Quieter=quieter | No change=no_change | Not sure=unsure

   - Regular — manual says regular is fine=regular_ok | Regular — manual recommends higher=regular_low | Premium / mid-grade=premium | Not sure=unsure

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

   - Low speeds only=low | Also on highway=highway_too | Not sure=unsure
