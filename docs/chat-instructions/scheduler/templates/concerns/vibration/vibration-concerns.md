# Vibration

-- Steering wheel shake at highway speed Checklist --
1. Does the shaking start at a specific speed, like around 50 or 60 mph?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does the shaking get better or go away if you speed up past that point?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. If you carefully let off the gas and coast, does the shaking stay the same?
   - Stays the same=same | Quiets down=quiets | Gets worse=worse | Not sure=unsure
4. [multi] Is the shake mostly in the steering wheel, or do you also feel it in your seat?
   - Steering wheel=steering | Seat=seat | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you hit a pothole, curb, or big bump recently?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Have you had new tires put on, tires rotated, or wheels balanced in the last few months?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the car pull to one side at the same time the shaking happens?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Vibration or pulsing when braking Checklist --
1. Does the shaking only happen when you press the brake pedal?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Do you feel the brake pedal pushing back up against your foot as you slow down?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Is it worse when slowing down from highway speeds, or when stopping from low speeds?
   - Slowing from highway speeds=highway | Stopping from low speeds=low | Both=both | Not sure=unsure
4. [multi] Do you feel the shake in the steering wheel, the seat, the brake pedal, or all three?
   - Steering wheel=steering | Seat=seat | Brake pedal=pedal | Not sure=unsure
5. Does it get worse after a long downhill drive or after towing something heavy?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Have you had any brake work done in the last year?
   - Yes — recently=recent | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Does the car pull to one side when you brake at the same time?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Shaking at idle while stopped Checklist --
1. Does the shaking happen when the car is sitting still in Drive or Reverse?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does it smooth out or get better when you shift into Park or Neutral?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Does it get noticeably worse when you turn the air conditioning on?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Is the check engine light on, flashing, or has it been on recently?
   - Flashing=flashing | Solid on=solid | Off=off | On recently=recent
5. Does the engine sound rough, sputtery, or like it's about to stall?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
6. Does it shake more when the engine is cold first thing in the morning, or after it warms up?
   - When cold (morning)=cold | After it warms up=warm | About the same=same | Not sure=unsure
7. Have you noticed any drop in gas mileage recently?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Shaking when speeding up or going uphill Checklist --
1. Does the shaking only happen when you're pressing the gas, and go away when you let off?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Is it worse when you're really pushing the engine, like passing on the highway or climbing a hill?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Do you hear any clicking or popping noises when turning, especially in tight parking lots?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Have you noticed any grease or oil splatter on the inside of your wheels?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
5. Does the shaking come and go, or is it there every time you accelerate?
   - Comes and goes=intermittent | Every time I accelerate=every | Not sure=unsure
6. [multi] Do you feel it more through the floor and seat than the steering wheel?
   - Floor=floor | Seat=seat | Steering wheel=steering | Not sure=unsure
7. Has the transmission been slipping or shifting strangely along with the shaking?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure

-- Shaking or bouncing over bumps and rough roads Checklist --
1. Does the car keep bouncing more than once or twice after going over a bump?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Do you hear a clunking or knocking noise when you hit bumps or dips?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. Does the car feel like it's wandering or hard to keep straight on uneven pavement?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
4. Is the ride a lot rougher than it used to be, even on roads that used to feel fine?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
5. Have you noticed any oily fluid leaking near the wheels or shock absorbers?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked
6. Does the front end dive down more than usual when you brake hard?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
7. Are your tires wearing unevenly, with bald spots or scalloped patches?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Haven't checked=unchecked

-- Constant vibration that doesn't change with speed Checklist --
1. Is the vibration there even when the car is barely moving, like in a parking lot?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
2. Does it stay roughly the same whether you're going 25 mph or 65 mph?
   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
3. [multi] Do you feel it more in the floor, the seat, or all over the car?

   - Yes=yes | No=no | Not sure=unsure
