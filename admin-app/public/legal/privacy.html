Privacy Policy

Jeff's Automotive Inc. · Effective 2026-05-30 · Updated 2026-07-01

This Privacy Policy explains how Jeff's Automotive Inc. ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our business systems, including our online appointment scheduler, our text (SMS) communications, and our QuickBooks Online integration application ("the App"). These are internal tools used to run Jeff's Automotive Inc.'s business operations.

1. Information we handle

Our systems work with:

  • Appointment and contact information. When you book service online or by phone we collect your name, mobile phone number, optional email address, vehicle information, and details about your service concern, so we can schedule and perform the work.
  • QuickBooks Online (QBO) accounting data. With your authorization through Intuit's OAuth process, the App reads and writes accounting data in your connected QBO company — for example customers, invoices, items, payments, and financial reports.
  • Business operations data. Customer contact details, vehicles, repair orders, appointments, and related shop records that we create and maintain to operate the business.

2. How we use information

We use this information only to operate Jeff's Automotive Inc.'s business — including scheduling, servicing vehicles, billing and accounting, customer communications, and internal reporting and analysis (which may include automated/AI-assisted analysis to help summarize or organize the data). We do not use it for advertising.

3. How information is shared

We do not sell your information. We use trusted third-party service providers to operate our systems — for example to host and store data, process and analyze information, and send communications on our behalf. These providers may only process information as needed to provide their services to us and are expected to protect it. We may also disclose information where required by law.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, excluding the messaging providers acting on our behalf solely to deliver our messages.

4. Text messaging (SMS)

With your consent — given by checking the consent box in our online appointment scheduler or verbally when booking by phone — we send transactional text messages: phone-number verification codes, appointment confirmations, and appointment reminders. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. See our Terms & Conditions for the full SMS program terms.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as the relevant subscription or integration is active, plus approximately 90 days, after which it is deleted or de-identified — except where we are required to keep records longer to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information, including encryption in transit and at rest and access controls. Access credentials and tokens used to connect to QBO are stored securely. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You can disconnect the App from QuickBooks Online at any time through your Intuit account, which stops further access to your QBO data. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of information we hold, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy; the "Effective" date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
